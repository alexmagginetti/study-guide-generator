# Study Guide Generator

A small, hand-authored kit of prompts that turns any modern AI chat — Claude, Gemini, or Grok — into a Bible-study or Christian-book-study generator for your small group, Sunday school class, or personal study. The kit is open-source in the sense that anyone can read every word the AI is told to do; it is not a black box.

**You will need.** A paid subscription to Claude (Pro or above), Gemini (Advanced), or Grok (Premium+ or SuperGrok). Free tiers will not produce a complete study. ChatGPT/OpenAI is intentionally not on the list — see GENERATOR.md, Section J.

**To use, in three steps.**

1. Open a fresh chat in your AI of choice.
2. Drag every file in this folder into the chat, *or* — if your AI can fetch URLs — paste this single sentence into the chat: *"Please read https://github.com/alexmagginetti/study-guide-generator/blob/main/GENERATOR.md and follow the instructions inside."*
3. Tell the AI: "I want a study on [whatever]." It will ask you four short questions, announce its defaults out loud for everything else, and produce a complete HTML study you can open in any browser, save to PDF, or email to your group.

**Trust posture.** Hand-authored by Alex Magginetti. Anchored to specific evangelical standards — the Lausanne Covenant baseline, with the Southern Baptist Convention / Dallas Theological Seminary toggle enabled by default. Every generated study should be reviewed by a separate AI session using the QA Agent Prompt in GENERATOR.md, Section F. That second pass is how factual errors and hallucinated original-language data get caught.
